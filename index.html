HUMAN RHYTHMS

Human Rhythms originated as a book documenting ambient sounds heard along Banker Street in Brooklyn over a series of mornings in November 2022. These sounds were translated into a visual score, organized by time and volume rather than melody or authorship.

This mobile-only website reinterprets that system through touch. Sounds and images are triggered intuitively, layered freely, and dissolved upon release. The result is a participatory, abstract compositionâ€”shaped momentarily by those who interact with it.

Touch to trigger a sound + image.

Press + hold to grow louder and larger.

Release to end the sound.

Multiple touches can overlap.
